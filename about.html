Peterlee Chess Club welcomes players of all abilities, from beginner to master. The only chess club in the areas, from Sedgefield to Sunderland. Peterlee Chess Club welcomes players of all standards, from beginner to master. We play every TUESDAY evening in the large Concert Hall of the Peterlee Catholic Club from 7.30pm onward. We have the whole room to ourselves, its spacious, well lit, heated and serves very reasonably priced ale for those wishing to partake!

There is ample parking to the rear and a small car park at the front of the club. We're always looking for new players and you're guaranteed a warm welcome from a friendly and knowledgeable group of players in Peterlee. We play every week of the year including Christmas and the New Year when dates are favourable! There is always a game waiting for you at Peterlee. There is also the chance to play league chess against clubs throughout Co Durham.
